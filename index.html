Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 23, 2026

Soul Read: Poker Trainer ("the App") is developed by Todd Sherrill. This policy describes how the App handles your information.

Data We Collect

None. Soul Read does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. The App runs entirely offline on your device.

Local Storage

The App stores the following data locally on your device using SharedPreferences:

  • Training session statistics (hands played, grades, accuracy scores)
  • Cumulative all-time statistics
  • Your hand preference settings

This data never leaves your device. It is not transmitted to any server, third party, or analytics service.

Network Access

Soul Read does not require an internet connection and makes no network requests. The App functions entirely offline.

Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, crash reporting, or tracking services.

Children's Privacy

The App does not collect any data from any user, including children. No personal information is gathered, stored remotely, or shared.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, we will post the revised version on this page with an updated date.
